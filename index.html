Lorde shared that she’s working on her third album

December 13, 2019December 16, 2019 Gail CraigUncategorized

Actress Lorde has confirmed that she is busy at the moment in creating music for her third album. The 22-year-old singer, Ella Marija Lani Yelich-O’Connor— her real name, has made an announcement on Instagram to mark the two-year anniversary of her celebrated 2017 LP Melodrama.

“I was such a baby-making that work, lots of emotions and learning so much all the time. Feels like I’ve grown a lot since then, I’ve been to Antarctica, I have a dog now and a cat and I can bake bread and cook dinner and keep plants alive, etc.

“It’s a good life you’ve given me. Thank you thank you. The third one in the oven,” Lorde wrote on Instagram.

The singer also thanked the fans for their continued support and love.

“Melodrama came out two years ago… I want to say thank you for how you took that record and made it your own. Swells my da*n heart thinking about your love for it and your willingness to go there with me and to dance the way that we did together.

“The day it came out I did a jigsaw puzzle all day in my hotel room in NY. I felt so empty, I didn’t really know yet that you make a record and get filled up, and then releasing it empties you and slowly fills you up again,” she added.

Lorde had been trained to be a star. She signed a training contract and started working on her craft after a recruiter saw videos of her performing at a high school talent show. She focused heavily on music, drawing influence and inspiration from her mother, who is a poet.

The focus on the lyrics and music of her songs will continue as she aged and become more popular. Notably, for her shows, the artist does not put a lot of work into dancing or choreography. She’s recognized for her profound lyrics and a haunting voice.
